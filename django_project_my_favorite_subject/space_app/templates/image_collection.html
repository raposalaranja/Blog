MARS PERSEVERANCE ROVER

    According to NASA, The Mars 2020 rover, Perseverance, is based on the Mars Science Laboratory's Curiosity rover configuration. It is car-sized, about 10 feet long (not including the arm), 9 feet wide, and 7 feet tall (about 3 meters long, 2.7 meters wide, and 2.2 meters tall). But at 2,260 pounds (1,025 kilograms), it weighs less than a compact car. In some sense, the rover parts are similar to what any living creature would need to keep it "alive" and able to explore.


JAMES WEBB SPACE TELESCOPE

    The U.S. Postal Service issued a stamp celebrating NASA’s James Webb Space Telescope, the largest, most powerful, and most complex science telescope ever put in space. The stamp, which features an illustration of the observatory, was dedicated in a ceremony Thursday, on September 8th, at the Smithsonian’s National Postal Museum in Washington.
